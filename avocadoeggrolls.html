Avocado Egg Rolls

These avocado egg rolls taste just likes the one you would order at a restaurant! Pair it with a sweet and tangy dipping sauce and appetizers are covered!


30 Minutes
12 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Ripe Avocados, cubed
  • 1/3 Cup Red Onion, diced
  • 1/3 Cup Sun Dried Tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 Cup Cream Cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon Cilantro, chopped
  • 2 Teaspoons Diced Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Chili Pepper
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • Egg Roll Wrappers
  • Vegetable Oil for Frying
  1. In a large bowl, add the diced avocado. Mash some pieces but also leave some chunks. 


  2. To the avocado, add the red onion, sun dried tomatoes, cream cheese, cilantro, chipotle peppers, and spices. Give it a gentle mix.


  3. Grab an egg roll wrapper. Add filling on the side closest to you, about 2 tablespoons. Roll over the filling to cover and then fold the sides over towards the center. Brush all the edges with water and finish rolling. Seal tightly. Repeat until there is no more filling. 


  4. Heat vegetable oil in a shallow pan until very hot (350F). Drop the egg rolls in and let the first side get golden brown before flipping over. Allow the other side to get golden brown. Remove from the oil and transfer to a paper towel to drain any excess oil. 

  5. Serve with a dipping sauce and enjoy!
